## Summary
Created a comprehensive Risk Management Dashboard for monitoring stablecoin exposure and risk metrics.

Full-featured risk management dashboard with the following components:

## Components Implemented

### 1. RiskOverview
Summary cards displaying:
- Overall Risk Score (0-100)
- Total Exposure (USD)
- Average Health Factor
- 24h Alert Count

### 2. DepegMonitor
Real-time stablecoin price deviation tracking:
- Current price vs peg price
- Deviation percentage
- 24h price change
- Mini sparkline chart (24h history)
- Risk level badges (LOW/MEDIUM/HIGH/CRITICAL)
- Supports: USDT, USDC, DAI, VNST

### 3. ExposureChart
Protocol allocation visualization:
- Interactive pie chart
- Protocol breakdown (Aave, Compound, Morpho, Yearn)
- Health factor per protocol
- Position counts
- Risk level indicators

### 4. ConcentrationRiskPanel
Portfolio diversification limits:
- By Token (USDC, USDT, DAI, VNST)
- By Chain (Ethereum, Arbitrum, Base, Optimism)
- Visual progress bars
- Limit thresholds
- Status indicators (OK/WARNING/EXCEEDED)

### 5. HealthFactorMonitorPanel
Liquidation risk monitoring:
- Health factor per position
- Liquidation threshold display
- Warning threshold display
- Position value
- Visual health gauge

### 6. AlertHistory
Recent risk events:
- Severity levels (INFO/WARNING/CRITICAL)
- Alert title and description
- Source identification
- Timestamps
- Acknowledge button
- Resolved status
